Daily Verse

Matthew 6:33 · ESV

"But seek first the kingdom of God and his righteousness, and all these things will be added to you."

Reflection

Jesus invites us to reorder our priorities — when we put God's kingdom above our own anxieties and ambitions, He promises to take care of everything else. This isn't passive; it's an active daily choice to trust Him first.

Pause & consider

What are you seeking first today — and how might this verse redirect your morning?

This week's verse

Psalm 119:105 · ESV

"Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path."

Context

Psalm 119 is the longest chapter in the Bible — a meditation on God's Word. This verse captures the essence of why scripture matters: it lights the path ahead even when we can't see far.

How to memorize it

Read it aloud 3 times. Then close your eyes and try to say it from memory. When you feel ready, tap Continue to test yourself — the verse will disappear.
